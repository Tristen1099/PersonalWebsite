Rock, Fern, Herbivor is like the classic 2 player game, rock, paper, scissors but with a twist! Each player chooses either rock, fern or herbivor. However there is a chance that a meteor will come and destroy all!

The possible outcomes:

  • Rock destroys the Herbivor.
  • Herbivor eats the fern.
  • Fern covers the rock.
  • Meteor destroys all!
